The FTA Regulatory Mandate

Under Federal Decree-Law No. 47 of 2022, the UAE Federal Tax Authority (FTA) mandates strict adherence to the Arm's Length Principle (ALP).

Secure Expense Deductibility (Article 28)

Expense deductibility is strictly contingent upon meeting the Market Value threshold. Without empirical backing, these payments risk immediate tax base adjustments and permanent disallowance.

* The Wholly and Exclusivity test must be performed annually across all transactions.

Prevent Costly Upward Adjustments

Failure to establish the ALP gives the FTA the statutory authority to execute an upward adjustment to your Taxable Income. If your expenses are deemed non-arm’s length, the excess amount is disallowed as a deduction—directly inflating your bill.

Comply with Articles 34, 35 & 36

We ensure your transactions with Related Parties and Connected persons reflect terms that independent parties would agree upon, shielding your business from base erosion penalties.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the AED 4,999 flat fee cover all of my company's intercompany transactions? +

No. The AED 4,999 fee covers the deep-dive analysis of one specific transaction category (e.g., just your intercompany loan, or just your KMP Remuneration). Different transactions require entirely different economic models and global search strategies. Therefore, each unique activity requires its own dedicated benchmark to ensure FTA compliance. However, closely linked transactions might be included.

What is the difference between this Benchmarking Report and a Transfer Pricing Local File? +

Think of the Benchmarking Report as the mathematical engine—it is the hard, data-driven proof of your pricing extracted from global markets. A Local File is the comprehensive, legally mandated compliance narrative required by Article 55 of the Corporate Tax Law. The Local File houses benchmarking data, but surrounds it with extensive qualitative analysis (FAR analysis, entity characterization, etc.). In short: The Benchmark is the mathematical proof; the Local File is your complete legal defense.

If I buy this report, are my UAE Transfer Pricing obligations fully resolved? +

It depends on your size. If your revenue exceeds AED 200 million or you are a Qualifying Free Zone Person (QFZP), you are legally required to maintain a full Master and Local File. However, even if you are below that threshold, Article 34 still requires you to prove your pricing is Arm's Length. This Benchmark is the critical, audit-ready weapon you need to defend your tax profile, regardless of your company's size.
